# Audio sources

## Bundled recordings

| App use | Recording | License | Why it fits |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Chord previews, arpeggios, picked and muted voices | [Karoryfer Emilyguitar](https://github.com/sfzinstruments/karoryfer.emilyguitar) | CC0 1.0 | Individual notes, multiple dynamics and articulations make pitch-correct chords possible. |
| Trainer, Song Coach, and Right-hand Studio clicks | [BigSoundBank Hi-hat close #5](https://bigsoundbank.com/hi-hat-close-5-s2300.html) by Joseph Sardin | CC0 | A short studio-recorded transient stays clear at practice tempos and can be redistributed in the app. |

The bundled files are local derivatives, so playback does not depend on a third-party host or a
network connection. Detailed transformations are documented beside each asset set in `public/`.

## Good future source

[FreePats Spanish classical guitar](https://freepats.zenvoid.org/Guitar/acoustic-guitar.html) is a
CC0, note-by-note nylon-string library. It is the best candidate if Chord Hero adds a distinct
classical-guitar voice later. It was not mislabeled as part of the current clean electric bank.
